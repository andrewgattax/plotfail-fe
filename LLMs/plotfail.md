## Project Overview

PlotFail is a mobile application built around humor, creativity, and interactive storytelling.

The core idea of the app is simple: users generate and complete absurd story templates by filling in missing words one placeholder at a time. These templates contain gaps such as nouns, verbs, adjectives, names, numbers, and other word categories. Once completed, the placeholders are inserted into the story to create unexpected and often hilarious results.

PlotFail combines elements of party games, casual creativity apps, and social sharing.

---

## Core Concept

A template is an incomplete story containing placeholders.

Example:

> I remember the day I met a {adjective} pirate during {season}.  
> He was holding a {noun} and screaming {verb}.

Users complete the template by entering custom words. The final result becomes a personalized story generated from their inputs.

The experience should feel fun, lightweight, surprising, and replayable.

---

## Main User Flows

## 1. Generate Template

Users can generate a new story template using AI.

The AI creates a unique template with a specific structure and placeholders, the user can choose to like the template and use it or discard it and generate a new one.

---

## 2. Fill Template

Users complete templates step by step.

Instead of seeing all placeholders at once, the app asks for one input at a time.

Example:
- Enter an adjective
- Enter a noun
- Enter a verb

This creates a game-like experience with anticipation and pacing.

---

## 3. Story Result

After all placeholders are filled, the final story is revealed.

The completed story should feel rewarding, funny, and shareable.

Users may:
- Save the story
- Replay the same template with different words
- Publish it publicly
- Share it

---

## 4. Saved Stories

Users can keep a personal collection of stories they completed.

Saved stories are private unless explicitly published.

---

## 5. Public Stories

Users can browse stories published by other users.

This creates a social layer where players can discover funny outcomes created from shared templates.

Potential interactions:
- Save the template
- Reuse template

---

## 6. User Profile

Each user has a profile/dashboard with simple statistics such as:

- Templates completed
- Stories saved
- Stories published
- Favorites or likes received

The profile should feel lightweight and playful, not overly serious.

---

## Product Personality

PlotFail should feel:

- Fun
- Fast
- Casual
- Social
- Slightly chaotic
- Meme-friendly
- Easy to understand instantly

It should never feel corporate, heavy, or overly complex.

---

## Design Philosophy

The app should prioritize:

- Short sessions
- Instant gratification
- Humor
- Replayability
- Clean mobile UX
- Smooth onboarding
- Low friction interactions

Users should be able to open the app and have fun within seconds, because human attention spans now resemble goldfish with internet access.

---

## Long-Term Vision

PlotFail can evolve beyond simple templates into a creative social platform where users generate, remix, publish, and compete through humor-driven storytelling.

The heart of the product is user creativity amplified by AI.
